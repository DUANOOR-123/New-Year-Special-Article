Let’s not focus on what you’ve lost,
What you’ve suffered,
How hard it was,
Or who was there with you.

Take a moment to pause, and reflect…

How much have you gained?
How have your struggles made you stronger?
How did Allah guide you through it all?

I know you’ve been broken into pieces many times.
There may have been no one to help,
or maybe you didn’t ask for it.
There were days when healing felt impossible,
Nights when you cried in silence.

But did you notice how you managed it all?
How you kept going, even when it was tough?
How did you grow stronger, even when you felt weak?

It was Allah by your side, always.
With every hardship, He brought you ease.
He comforted you in ways you may not have noticed.

So, take a moment to Thank Allah and all the blessings He has given you
For the growth, the lessons, and everything you have.
Recognize them, appreciate them, and release any negativity.

Wishing you the best as you step into new beginnings.
